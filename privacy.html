Privacy Policy
Effective March 25, 2026  ·  JC Laboratories / jclabs.tech
JC Laboratories is built on a simple principle: your data belongs to you. We collect the minimum information required to operate our services, and we design our systems so encrypted content remains unreadable to us.

Who We Are

JC Laboratories (jclabs.tech) is an independent technology company founded by Justin Czap. We develop security-focused software tools including Aleph Vault, CipherPort, Cryptaverse, the EGAF Framework, and Gaffer.

Our research and development division, JC Labs R&D, develops the Entropy-Gated AI Framework (EGAF) and related products. Our mission is to put real security tools in the hands of real people, without compromise.

Content vs Metadata — What We Can and Cannot See

JC Laboratories is designed so that we cannot read your encrypted content.

What we cannot access:

  • Vault contents such as notes, passwords, API keys, and private attachments
  • Private cryptographic keys stored in your vault
  • Encrypted message and attachment contents
  • Encrypted group content protected client-side
  • Cloud backup contents once encrypted on your device

What we do process and store as service metadata:

  • Account information such as email address, account status, and subscription tier
  • Public profile or listing information that you choose to publish
  • Public keys or public key material needed for discovery or routing, where applicable
  • Message routing information such as recipient identifiers, timestamps, and expiry settings
  • Encrypted message blobs and encrypted backup blobs as unreadable ciphertext
  • Operational data such as request counts, abuse-prevention signals, billing state, and backup metadata

We do not use this data for advertising, profiling, or resale. We use it only to operate, secure, and support the service.

Our Products

Aleph Vault

Aleph Vault is an offline-first encrypted vault application for Android. It provides secure local storage for notes, passwords, cryptographic key pairs, API keys, and encrypted messaging via the MessageCrypt system.

All sensitive vault data is encrypted on-device before storage. On Free and Individual tiers, vault data remains on your device unless you choose to export it. On Family, Business, and Enterprise tiers, encrypted cloud backup blobs may be stored by us after being encrypted on your device. We cannot decrypt those blobs.

Device model: Aleph Vault is designed primarily for single-device use. Additional devices are supported through encrypted backup and restore, not real-time multi-device synchronization.

EGAF — Entropy-Gated AI Framework

EGAF is a structured AI governance protocol developed by JC Labs R&D. It defines a governed composition system for auditable AI task execution.

EGAF is licensed under CC BY-NC 4.0 for non-commercial use. Commercial use requires written permission from JC Laboratories.

Gaffer — EGAF AI Assistant

Gaffer is the flagship AI assistant built on EGAF. It provides governed AI interactions through a web interface and API.

Messages sent through Gaffer may be processed by third-party AI providers such as Anthropic, Groq, and OpenAI, subject to their own privacy policies. JC Laboratories may retain limited operational metadata necessary for rate limiting, abuse prevention, and service reliability, but we do not market Gaffer as an end-to-end encrypted or zero-knowledge product.

Third-party providers: Anthropic, Groq, OpenAI.

CipherPort and Cryptaverse

CipherPort is the community gateway of JC Laboratories and the public-facing entry point into Cryptaverse.

Cryptaverse is a privacy-first cryptographic social platform in which content is encrypted client-side before reaching our servers. We cannot read encrypted content. We do, however, process limited metadata needed to authenticate users, route messages, enforce quotas, and operate the platform.

CipherPort may include public-facing features such as public listings, public billboards, public group boards, and encrypted relay features. Any information you choose to make public through those features is not private.

Encrypted relay features: For dead-drop or relay-style messaging, JC Laboratories operates as a blind courier. We transport encrypted ciphertext and cannot read its contents, but we may process minimal routing metadata such as recipient identifiers, timestamps, and expiry.

Vault Password, Account Password, and Recovery

Your account password and vault password are separate.

Account password: used to access your JC Laboratories account. This may be reset through our authentication provider using standard account recovery procedures.

Vault password: used locally on your device to derive encryption keys that protect private vault material at rest. JC Laboratories does not store or know your vault password.

On standard tiers, if you forget your vault password and do not have a valid backup or export, your vault contents may be permanently unrecoverable. This is by design.

Enterprise Recovery Program

Opt-in only. Enterprise Recovery is not enabled by default. It applies only to users who explicitly enroll under a separate agreement or service arrangement.
  • Recovery services require explicit enrollment
  • Recovery may require identity verification such as government-issued ID, video call, or other agreed method
  • Recovery events may be logged for audit and abuse prevention
  • Opting out ends participation and associated retained recovery records are deleted according to our retention schedule and legal obligations

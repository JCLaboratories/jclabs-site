5. Vault Password — Standard Tiers

Free, Individual, and Family Tiers — No Recovery: Aleph Vault uses zero-knowledge encryption. Your vault password derives your encryption key locally on your device. JC Laboratories does not store, transmit, or have access to your vault password or its derived key. If you forget your vault password on these tiers, your encrypted vault data cannot be recovered by anyone — including JC Laboratories. There is no reset, no recovery code, and no backdoor.

You acknowledge and accept this limitation by using standard tiers. We strongly recommend exporting your vault regularly and storing the backup securely.

6. Vault Export and Import

Available on Individual tier and above. Vault export creates an encrypted backup file protected by a user-chosen export password — separate from and independent of the vault master password. The export password is chosen by you at the time of export and is not stored by JC Laboratories.

Your export password cannot be recovered. If you lose your export password, the backup file cannot be decrypted. Store your export password separately from the backup file.

Exported files are saved to your device's Downloads/AlephVault/ folder. You are solely responsible for the security of exported vault files after export.

Restore from backup is available on Individual tier and above. After signing in, users with a valid backup file may restore their vault instead of creating a new one.

7. Cloud Backup — Business and Enterprise Tiers

Business and Enterprise tier subscribers may enable automatic encrypted cloud backup. Vault data is encrypted on-device before upload. JC Laboratories stores only the encrypted blob and cannot decrypt it. Cloud backup enables restore on any device after sign-in without requiring a local backup file.

Encrypted blobs are retained while the subscription is active and deleted 30 days after subscription cancellation.

9. JC Labs Community — Dead Drop Board

The dead drop board is an anonymous encrypted message relay. No account is required to post.

JC Laboratories operates the dead drop board as a blind courier. All messages posted to the board must be encrypted ciphertext. We cannot read posted messages. We are not responsible for the content of encrypted messages we cannot decrypt.

Message expiry: Senders choose an expiry time at posting. Free and anonymous users: up to 7 days. Paid tier account holders: up to 30 days. Messages are automatically and permanently deleted at expiry.

Posting requirements: Messages posted to the dead drop board must be valid encrypted ciphertext. Posting unencrypted plaintext, unencrypted personal data, illegal content in readable form, or spam is a violation of these Terms.

Law enforcement: We will comply with legally valid requests. However, we can only provide the encrypted ciphertext — which cannot be decrypted without the recipient's private key, which never leaves their device. We maintain no logs of poster IP addresses or identities.

6. Account Password vs Vault Password

Aleph Vault uses separate credentials with different security properties.

Account password: used to sign in to your JC Laboratories account. This may be reset through our authentication flow.

Vault password: your vault password is used locally on your device to derive encryption keys protecting private vault material at rest. JC Laboratories does not store or know your vault password. On standard tiers, if you forget it and do not have a valid backup or export, your encrypted vault data may be permanently unrecoverable.

By using Aleph Vault, you acknowledge and accept this limitation.

7. Vault Export and Import

Export and import features may be available on certain tiers. Vault export creates an encrypted backup file protected by a user-chosen export password separate from the vault password unless explicitly stated otherwise in the product.

Your export password may be unrecoverable. If you lose it, the backup file may be permanently unreadable.

You are solely responsible for the security of exported files once they leave the application.

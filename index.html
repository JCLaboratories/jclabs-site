// security model

What we cannot do
is a feature

Our security model is defined by what we deliberately chose not to build.

Zero Knowledge Vault

Your vault password derives your encryption key locally. It never leaves your device. We cannot read your vault under any circumstances.

No Recovery — By Design

Free, Individual, and Family tiers have no password recovery. Gone is gone. This is not a bug — it's the security guarantee that makes the rest possible.

Blind Courier

Dead drop messages are encrypted before they reach our servers. We route ciphertext we cannot decrypt. We are not responsible for content we cannot see.

No Telemetry

Aleph Vault collects no analytics, no usage data, no crash reports. Your account email and tier are the only things we know about you.

X25519 + Ed25519

MessageCrypt uses elliptic curve Diffie-Hellman key exchange with Ed25519 digital signatures. Every message is authenticated and encrypted.

Open Crypto Primitives

AES-256-GCM, HKDF, PBKDF2. Industry-standard algorithms — no proprietary cryptography, no security through obscurity.
